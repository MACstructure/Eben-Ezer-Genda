Biography

The Journey of Dr. Genda

Dr. Eben Ezer Genda is a highly accomplished surgeon whose career is defined by a dual commitment to clinical precision and global healthcare reform. Born from a desire to make advanced surgical care a universal standard rather than a regional privilege, Dr. Genda has spent the last decade refining his expertise in Cardiac and Global Surgery.

"Medicine is more than a series of procedures; it is a sacred trust between a physician and a community to uphold the dignity of every life through science and empathy."

Throughout his biography, one theme remains constant: Health Equity. Dr. Genda’s background spans multiple continents, from intensive surgical residencies in world-class trauma centers to leading mobile surgical units in underserved regions. This breadth of experience allows him to navigate complex clinical challenges with a perspective that is both technically rigorous and culturally sensitive.

Today, he serves as a lead consultant and researcher, focusing on how innovative surgical technologies can be adapted for low-resource environments without compromising on outcomes. His life’s work is dedicated to ensuring that the heart of the patient remains at the center of the medical progress.
